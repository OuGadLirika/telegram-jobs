This Privacy Policy explains how [telegram-jobs] (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Telegram bot and related services (the “Service”).

Who This Service Is For
The Service is intended for Telegram channel owners, creators, and administrators who manage job channels and want to automate job collection, moderation, and publishing.

Information We Collect
We may collect and process the following data:

Telegram account data: user ID, username, display name, and chat/channel identifiers.

Content data: messages, commands, and job post data submitted to or processed by the bot.

Operational data: timestamps, moderation actions, queue status, publishing history, and delivery results.

Technical data: error logs, diagnostic logs, and basic usage metadata needed for reliability and security.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you provide it in content submitted to the bot.

How We Use Information
We use collected information to:

Operate core bot features (parsing, queueing, moderation, and publishing).

Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot technical issues.

Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.

Comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Legal Basis (where applicable)
If GDPR or similar laws apply, we process data based on:

Performance of a service requested by the user.

Legitimate interests (security, maintenance, and improvement).

Legal obligations.

Data Sharing

A successor entity in case of merger, acquisition, or asset transfer.

Data Retention
We keep data only as long as needed for operational purposes, moderation history, security, and legal compliance.
When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it within a reasonable period.
